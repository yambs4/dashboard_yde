## Task 3: Proposed Required Expertise and Core Skills Mapping

Based on my analysis of all 27 projects, here's my proposed mapping:

### Proposed Required Expertise Categories:
1. **Monitoring & Evaluation** - Projects focusing on monitoring frameworks, progress tracking
2. **Impact Evaluation** - Projects using PSM, DiD, RCT, quasi-experimental designs
3. **Baseline Assessment** - Initial situation analyses and baseline surveys
4. **Business Analytics** - Financial modeling, KPI development, business planning
5. **Economic Research** - Policy research, CGE modeling, econometric analysis
6. **Formative/Process Evaluation** - Mid-term assessments, learning evaluations
7. **Terminal Evaluation** - End-of-project impact assessments
8. **Data Quality Assurance** - Survey management, data validation
9. **Capacity Building** - Training, mentorship, technical assistance
10. **Environmental Economics** - Natural resource management, WTP studies
11. **Nutrition & Health** - Health program evaluations, nutrition assessments
12. **Migration & Climate Change** - Mobility studies, disaster risk assessment
