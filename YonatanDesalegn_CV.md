**Desalegn, Y. (2020).** Off-farm Activities, Incomes and Household Welfare in Rural Ethiopia.

Jönköping University and Addis Ababa University

**Desalegn, Y. (2018).** Returns to Education in Ethiopia. In: Heshmati, A., Yoon, H. (eds) Economic Growth and Development in Ethiopia. Perspectives on Development in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) Region

This study uses the 2013 National Labor Force Survey to measure marginal private returns to education in Ethiopia, finding 14.43% returns per year of schooling and 0.5% per year of experience. It identifies non-linearity and sheepskin effects in education returns.
